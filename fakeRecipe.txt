Ramen with shrimps

Ramen with shrimps, red and black pepper, and garlic

Noodles - 400g
Shrimps - 500g
Salt - 10g
Red pepper - 200g
Black pepper - 20g
Garlic - 2 heads

Put 4 liters of water on fire
Salt the water
Put noodles in
Cook for 8 minutes
Cut red pepper into little slices
After 8 minutes done, drain water
Put 2 liters of water in the pot
Put shrimps in
Salt the water
Put red pepper in
Cook for 15 minutes
After 15 minutes done, put noodles in
Cook for 5 minutes
